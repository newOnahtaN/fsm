The big white box above is the RBD designer.  Here's how to use it:

  • Add a block: double-click on the canvas
  • Connect two blocks with edge: shift-drag on a block
  • Move something: drag it around
  • Delete something: click it and press the delete key (not the backspace key)
  • Toggle edges to be straight or angled: double-click on the edge
  • Type numeric subscript: put an underscore before the number (like "S_0")
  • Type greek letter: put a backslash before it (like "\beta")
  • Toggle a block into joint: hold down the ctrl key and double-click on the block
    • - what is a joint? Are your lines not doing what you want them to do? Joints are used to visually manage edges since they act like a normal blocks, but go invisible when your mouse leaves the canvas.

This was made in HTML5 and JavaScript using the canvas element.
